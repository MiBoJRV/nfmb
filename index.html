Cyber Tools, Software & Resources for Financial Fraud Recovery

We deploy cutting-edge cyber tools, software, and resources to bolster our efforts in financial fraud recovery.

These tools are essential for conducting thorough investigations, gathering digital evidence, and analyzing complex financial transactions.

  • EnCase Forensic

    A digital forensic tool used for collecting, analyzing, and preserving digital evidence from computers and mobile devices

  • Palantir Gotham

    Data integration and analysis platform that enables investigators to connect disparate data sources and uncover patterns in financial fraud cases.

  • Cellebrite UFED

    Mobile forensic tool used to extract and analyze data from smartphones, helping in uncovering evidence related to financial fraud.

  • Thomson Reuters CLEAR

    Investigative platform providing access to public records, social media data, and other sources to aid in identifying and verifying individuals involved in financial fraud activities.

  • IBM i2 Analyst’s Notebook

    Visual analysis software that helps in detecting and disrupting financial fraud networks by analyzing complex data sets.
